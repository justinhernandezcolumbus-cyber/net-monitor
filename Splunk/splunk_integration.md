Overview

Home internet performance is difficult to evaluate without objective data. Anecdotal impressions of slow speeds or dropped connections are hard to quantify, and it's even more difficult to compare performance across different connection types within a household.

I need to be able to measure the actual performance of my network with real metrics to understand the quality of my service, and to determine whether to continue with my current provider. Should I get a new provider, is the service matching what my provider advertises, and what times of day is it the worst?

I am using Python to build a  CSV file, which will serve as a source for Splunk Enterprise for easy analysis, using SPL queries.
